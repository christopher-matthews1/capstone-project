About

Bootsville Kickball Club started in 2005 with just a bunch of high school graduates wanting to play competitive kickball. Then we only had one league with four teams but as the years went on our popularity grew and everyone wanted to join in on the fun we were having. Now we have leagues in four different locations to better suit the demand for teams and the convenience for our players. If this sounds like a good time to you, go ahead and sign up for a league close to you!
